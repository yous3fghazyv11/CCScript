* Script with objections
*format info:*

in this text i will be using the following:
- header lines: for specifying the context we're talking about
- normal text: for the normal agent speech not objection handling (think of stuff like the intro for example or asking about the condition, asking price, etc..)
- /italic text/: for rebuttals (stuff you reply with when you face an objections)
- smaller header lines: prospect's objections
- =coded words=: for notes

** Intro
Hi [prospect name]?

This is Alex, i was calling in regards to a property i believe you own on [street name], i was just reaching out to see if you might be open to get a cash offer on it?
=be confident, energetic and curious=

*** not interested

/alright so do you happen to have any other properties that you might consider selling, right now or in the near future?/
=don't push, we're not sales, ask politely and show curiosity=

*** not right now

/so if you were to sell at some point, is that something you would be thinking about months from now? years from now?/

*** what's your offer?

/So generally these calls take about five to seven minutes, and i wanna learn more about the property and your specific situation to be able to give you a well educated offer, and if you're a good fit for what we specialize in i will let you know at the end of the call as well as setting up a followup call to give you an offer/
=So this establishes  the structure of the call already, they know what they're about to go through, and they will only continue of they're serious about it, this saves you time by filtering window shoppers (people who just want to hear and offer)=

*** who are u, how did you get that info

/I'm representing the cash buyers here, they're a group of private investors and they're few properties in your area right now; they took interest in your property, so they wanted me to call and see if you would consider selling it anytime soon./

*** Where are you located

=yet to be found an answer for=

** condition
Q1# so i believe it has 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, correct?

Q2# and have you done any repairs or renovations that you would like me to know about?
=Let them talk, that's good, the more you open up, the more they reveal stuff you can use against them later=

Q3# alright and is there anything that i need to be spending money on right now in the property?

*** tell me your offer first / I'm not sharing any details

=This is a huge sign that they just wanna hear an offer, handle this carefully. But honestly if they said this, the call is probably hopeless=

/alright [prospect name], so i believe as you mentioned you might consider selling the house, and obviously you're not willing to get rid of just for nothing, and you know that in order for someone to give you an offer, it can't just be a random number and it has to be based on evidence, unless it's a big waste of your time/

/so the thing is, without basic property details, I can't responsibly estimate anything. So just so I don’t waste your time, Can you give me a rough break down about the condition?/

*** you didn't do your homework

/yes we did our homework, however I just like to confirm the info i got in person with you to make sure our offer is reasonable/

** occupancy
so do you currently live there? or is it rented out?

*** why is that important

/that's a good question frankly, it's because it's gonna affect the closing time, so in case it's rented for example, we're gonna need to move the tenants out first and stuff like that, so i just wanna make sure that the closing is going to be smooth and we will be able to do it asap/

** closing
so if we agree on an offer, how soon you think you'll be able to get paid and walk away from that property

=we can do maximum 3 months=

** asking price
and how much are you asking for this house right now?

=asking price needs to be lower than the market value=

*** high asking price

/so how did you come up with that price?/

=after whatever they say=

/yeah, fair, if i owned it i would like to get the highest number for it as well. But, so let me just tell you that we take the property as it is, which means that you're not going to spend anything updating the property/. /so with that in mind, what would you call a bottom line for this property?/

*** still high

/Alright fair! That sounds reasonable to me. So there's also one more thing that i would like to bring into your attention, so we're cash buyers, which means that we're paying all immediately in cash, which also means that —unlike realtors— there will be no **realtor fees** or **closing coasts**, so basically whatever your asking price is, that is going to be what goes in your pocket when you're walking away from that property/

/So, [prospect name], what is the least you are eager to get in-cash when walking away?/

** motivation
Alright perfect, That is pretty much all that i need....

But actually I'm kind of curios about something.

so i know i called with a cash offer for the property, but why did you consider getting rid of it?

*** money

/yea ofc the money is the most important factor and all, however/

- *occupied:* /this is where you live, the property's in a decent condition, so apart from the money factor and me calling you ofc, what makes you open to do so?/
- *rented:* /this is a property that you rent out and you're already making a cash flow passively out of it, so apart from the money factor and me calling you ofc, what makes you open to do so?/
- *vacant:* /since the property is vacant & i can see it is in a really decent area, what made you open to selling while you can rent it out and make some income off of that/

*** still money

/so do you have any future plans after selling the property?/

*** still no valid reason

/so we're looking for motivated sellers so i just wanted to make sure that you would def like to sell the house/
